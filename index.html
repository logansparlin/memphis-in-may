24 straight years?

We're talking about the world's most prestigious BBQ battleground—the Memphis in May World Championship Barbecue Cooking Contest. And for the past 24 years, every Grand Champion has built their legend on the performance and flavor of Cattlemen's® BBQ Sauce.

Seriously Epic BBQ

This year, more than 250 BBQ teams from near and far assembled on the banks of the Mississippi River to take their shot at the 2015 title. Cattlemen's® was there in full support with our variety of BBQ Sauces to fuel their inspiration. We were proud to crown winners in categories that included Shoulder, Whole Hog, and of course the Grand Champion - awarded to The Shed BBQ from Ocean Springs, Mississippi.

Grand Champion

For the past 24 years, every Grand Champion Team has built its legend on the performance and flavor of Cattlemen’s® BBQ Sauce. Congratulations to the 2015 Grand Champion, The Shed BBQ from Ocean Springs, Mississippi.

Three Days of BBQ

The Cattlemen's® hospitality tent was at the center of the BBQ buzz hosting more than 80 foodservice professionals. French's Food Foodservice Culinary Director, Chef Trip Kadey, treated our guests to three nights of outstanding BBQ dinners showcasing traditional, farm-to-fork, and global BBQ applications all featuring Cattlemen's® BBQ Sauce. But the BBQ fest didn't stop there. The Cattlemen's® crew and guests participated in a Hog Crawl, going behind the scenes to see how Champion Teams make Cattlemen's® the secret to their success.
